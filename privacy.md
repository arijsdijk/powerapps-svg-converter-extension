# Privacy Policy

This browser extension ("PowerApps SVG Converter") is designed to run entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What We Do (and Don’t) Collect
- No account data, personal information, or identifiers are collected.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is performed.
- No data is sent to external servers; all processing happens locally in your browser.

## How Your Data Is Used
- SVG content you upload or paste is processed locally to generate Power Apps–compatible output.
- The clipboard is only written to when you explicitly click a copy button.
- The extension does not persist data between sessions.

## Permissions
- Clipboard access: used solely to copy the converted output at your request.
- File access (via file picker): used only to read the SVG you select; files are not uploaded.

## Third‑Party Services
- The extension does not integrate with or send data to third‑party services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.
